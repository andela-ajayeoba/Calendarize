No obligation trial

You can try out Calendarize for free for 30 days. At any point during your trial, you can upgrade to one of our paid plans. If you choose not to, just let your trial run out, you will not be billed anything. You never need to worry about payment unless you explicitly provide your payment details.

What do I do if I want to upgrade?

If you want to upgrade to one of our paid plans, you can do so from the account settings page inside the application. After you have provided your payment details, you will be billed monthly until you choose to terminate your subscription. You will receive a final bill upon termination, whereafter no further billing will occur.

When will I be billed?

After your free trial, you will be billed on a monthly basis.

Can I cancel my account?

Yes, you can cancel your account at any time. After you've cancelled, you won't be billed again. If you cancel during your trial, you won't be billed at all.

I have more questions before I sign up!

You're always welcome to contact us at support@calendarize.com if you have any questions about the service, or about any of our plans or payment processes.
